About Us

Who We Are

Sediba Sa Bophelo Prayer Tower is a registered organization with the Department of Social Development (Reg No: 233-628 NPO).
Tax No: 9100231274  |  CSD No: MAAA1268467

Founded on the pillars of prayer, healing, and community support, our journey began with a vision to create a spiritual home where everyone is welcome, and where faith is lived out through action, compassion, and unity. We are committed to empowering individuals and families, nurturing spiritual growth, and serving our community with love and integrity.

Vision

Preaching the Gospel of change through the word of God and transforming the community with development.

Mission

Gathering the people of one mind, in one place, with one heart of building the nation through social cohesion and economic development.

Objectives

  • To preach the good news of the Gospel.
  • To take care of the needy and the underprivileged.
  • To conduct Gender Based Violence awareness programs.
  • To offer counseling sessions for victims of abuse and past trauma.
  • To enhance social, spiritual, and esthetic youth development.
  • To provide a supportive environment for individuals needing temporary care, support, and engagement.
  • To provide quality care and foundational education to young children in a safe environment.
  • To create awareness, provide support, and create a safer community for all.
  • To enhance learning outcomes and provide additional academic support to students.
  • To alleviate poverty through job creation and skills development.
  • To cultivate integrity, leadership, and moral values in our community.
