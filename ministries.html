Ministries

Our Ministries

  • Church Services: Inspiring worship, prayer, and teaching for all ages every week.
  • Multipurpose Center: A hub for community gatherings, events, and holistic development.
  • Drop In Center: Providing a safe, supportive space for those in need of care and engagement.
  • Day Care (ECD/Children Ministry): Quality early childhood development and spiritual nurturing in a safe environment.
  • Gender Based Violence and Femicide: Awareness, prevention, and support programs for a safer community.
  • Tutorial Classes: Academic support and skills development for students to enhance learning outcomes.
  • Expanded Public Works Program: Creating job opportunities and empowering the community through skills training.
  • Community Development: Initiatives to uplift, empower, and transform our local community.
  • School of Integrity: Cultivating leadership, moral values, and ethical living among youth and adults.
  • Counseling/Psychological: Professional counseling and support for individuals and families facing challenges.
  • Agricultural: Promoting food security and sustainable livelihoods through agricultural projects.
  • Sports, Art and Culture: Encouraging talent, teamwork, and cultural expression through various activities.
  • Women's Fellowship: Empowering women to grow spiritually and support one another in faith and life.
  • Outreach & Healing: Bringing hope, healing, and practical support to our community and beyond.
